# scion
Using the scion cli tool, start three agents, for the colors RGB, to each write a poem about their color. Block and wait for them patiently to notify you when they are done. Once you have been notified by all three agents that they have completed, find their poems in the workspace and composite them into anthology.md Finally delete the agents you started.
